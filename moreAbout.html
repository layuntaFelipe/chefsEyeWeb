More About the Software

What is it about

ChefsEye company is about making it easy to manage a restaurant, its customers, its storage, profit, and employees. All of that with the software on your computer or smartphone.

With the software, you have the power to create an interactive menu for your customers, a screen for your chef in the kitchen to keep track of the orders, and a screen of management of everything for the manager.

Who is it for?

ChefsEye is software made mainly for restaurant managers, owners, and chefs in the kitchen. So their jobs are easier to be done and managed.

How does it work?

ChefsEye works having in mind three main points: Storage Management, Automate Tasks, and Increase Profit.

By having a database it is possible to keep track of orders from the user by the interactive menu on their table. It automates tasks ass orders made by customers to their table, the income of food storage, and how much of each item is subtracted from each order made by the customer.

Storage Management

With the ChefsEye database, you will have the possibility to keep track of your income from food storage, the outcome of food storage, orders, and employment payments.

Automate Tasks

With the software, you will have the possibility to automate boring and repetitive tasks like employment payment, income food storage, outcome food storage, favorite dish, calculate profit, and make better decisions.

Increase Profits

With all the data you get from the software it's up to you as a manager to make the best use of it, but even by just having all of that data on your screen, the increased profit part is just a case of time.
