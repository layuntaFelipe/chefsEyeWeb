CREATION

How We Started

The company was started in a college room located in Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada, by a CIT (Computer Information Technology) student named Felipe in a User-Interface Design class.

Felipe as a Brazilian always loved good food and hanging out with his friends at a bar or restaurant.

He noted that every bar and restaurant had a QR Code menu, but it wasn’t an interactive menu, only a PDF page, and that’s when he came up with the software idea.
