More About CheffsEye Co.

Who started it

The company was started in a college room located in Lethbridge, Alberta, Canada, by a CIT (Computer Information Technology) student named Felipe in a User-Interface Design class.

Felipe always loved good food and hanging out with his friends at a bar or restaurant. He noted that every bar and restaurant had a QR Code menu, but it wasn’t an interactive menu, only a PDF page, and that’s when he came up with the software idea.

Problems Faced

One of the problems faced by the company was that it was only one person in control of designing, marketing, and coding. Juggling all of that with college responsibility.

To make that a possibility, time management was one of the biggest, if not the biggest focus while building the software and going to college.

Future Plans/Projects

The first plan of the company is to achieve enough customers to get famous around the restaurant and so increase the number of softwares sold.

With that money, the second step is to increase the number of employees in the company and buy a physical office.
